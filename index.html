🚖 Uber Pickup Analysis in NYC

Author: Fnu Gitanjali | LinkedIn

This project explores Uber pickup data in New York City using clustering, mapping, and time-based analysis techniques to uncover patterns and strategic insights.

🔍 Project Highlights

  • Analyzed over 14 million records from NYC Uber pickups
  • Applied KMeans, DBSCAN, OPTICS, and BIRCH clustering to discover geographic trends
  • Visualized data using Folium heatmaps and time series charts
  • Identified surge hours and borough-level pickup demand

📊 Tools & Technologies

  • Languages: Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
  • Clustering: Scikit-learn
  • Mapping: Folium
  • Notebook: Jupyter

🌟 Key Takeaways

  • Manhattan remains the core pickup zone, but outer boroughs are growing ~10%
  • Evening rush hours and late weekends show surge demand
  • Clustering reveals entertainment zones and commuter hotspots
